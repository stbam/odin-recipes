Lasagna

A simple dish designed for pizza lovers by pizza lovers in an all new form!

Lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta. Lasagna can refer to either the type of noodle or to the typical lasagna dish which
is a dish made with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients, such as meats and cheese, in between the lasagna noodles.

Ingredients

  • flat rectangle noodles
  • riccota cheese
  • meat
  • sauce

Steps

  1. cook noodle
  2. cook meat
  3. place noodle then meat then sauce
  4. consume
