Burger

Description

Shortened in length, the term Burger is used to describe a popular sandwich made from ground meats that are formed into a patty,
cooked, and placed between two halves of a bun. Although the most common Burger is made with meat, there are many alternatives
that do not include meat, such as tofu or ground vegetables.

ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • bread
  • cheese
  • onions

Steps

  1. buy beef
  2. cook beef cut onion
  3. place cheese on hot cooked beef
  4. assemble the burger
